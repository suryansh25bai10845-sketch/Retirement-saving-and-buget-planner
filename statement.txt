Problem statement :-

Individuals often struggle to estimate how much they need to save for retirement and how to manage their monthly budget effectively. This project aims to provide a Python-based tool that helps users calculate projected retirement savings using compound interest and analyze monthly income and expenses to support better financial planning.

Scope of the project:-

The project focuses on helping users plan their long-term retirement savings and manage monthly budgets through simple Python-based calculations. It provides a foundation for expanding into more advanced financial planning tools in the future.

Target users:-

The target users are individuals who want to manage their monthly expenses and plan for long-term retirement savings. It is especially useful for students, early professionals, and anyone beginning financial planning.

Here are 5 high-level features of the project:

1.	Calculates projected retirement savings using compound interest.
2.	Allows customization of age, savings, monthly contributions, and return rate.
3.	Provides a detailed monthly budget analysis based on user-defined expenses.
4.	Computes total expenses and remaining savings automatically.
5.	Offers a simple, easy-to-use Python framework for basic financial planning.
